This week our Lunch & Learn webinar will be an inspiring interview by Microverse CEO & Founder, Ariel Camus, with Microverse graduate - and now employee - Alvaro Sanchez. Alvaro completed the Microverse program last year and was hired by Microverse to work in the development team. Alvaro has worked on everything from bug fixes to implementing and deploying new tools that students use daily! During the interview, Alvaro will share his personal journey to becoming a junior web developer. Ariel will also ask him about his experience as a student at Microverse, and how he's enjoying his new career in software development. Wondering what you can expect in your first job as a software developer? Join the webinar and ask Alvaro anything!
